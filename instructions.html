Welcome to Thawed!

An adaptation of the popular Hangman word game

Follow the instructions below:

Enter a secret word in the form and click the submit button.

Have your opponent guess the mystery word by selecting unique letters from a series of alphabet buttons.

For each incorrect guess, a single part of the snowman disappears.

You must guess the correct word before the snowman melts.

Don't defrost The Code Freeze!
